Chic Secret

Simple password manager that respects your privacy

Chic Secret is a software totally open-source and free of any kind of trackers. The passwords can be synchronized with our server, self hosted or kept totally offline.

Available on android and IOS, you can keep your passwords safely in your pockets.

We generate passwords composed of words from a language we can understand making it easier for us without compromising with security.
Create our own categories with a title an icon and a color, making easy to find your passwords is our main concern.
Passwords are things we need on all your devices to connect on our services easily without compromising with security so adopt our mobile applications.
An easy access to our passwords is essential that's why we offer to unlock our vaults of passwords with our visage or fingerprints.
Open-source
The code of Chic Secret for the server and the application is fully available on GitHub under the GPL-3 license.

It means anybody can read and check the code to ensure we do not track you or save personal information for business purposes.

It also gives you the right to modify the code and redistribute it or to self host your server in order to keep everything under your own infrastructure.
