Password Manager

Chic Secret is an open source password manager available on Windows/Macos/Android/iOS and soon Linux.

The goal of Chic Secret is to give everyone the opportunity to safely save their passwords and be proactive about their online privacy.

Open Source

Open source guarantee your data and your privacy is safe and not used for any commercial use. We believe in transparency to gather trust around a product that serve the user's interest.

Desktop Application

Manage and organize your passwords through customizable categories powered by an outstanding search system.

Synchronize your passwords with all your devices and share your vaults with your friends and family.

Smartphone

You are outside and can't access your computer but you need this important password? No problem, Chic Secret also exists on smartphone and tablets with all the features of the Desktop application and even more.

Use your fingerprints or your face to unlock all your vaults and get an easy access to all your passwords.
